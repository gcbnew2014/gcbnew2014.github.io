C:Benefits To The Cardholder

Among the numerous benefits of the GCB MasterCard Debit cards to customers are:

Wider Access to funds: With other banks accepting the MasterCard brands here in Ghana as well as the millions of acceptance locations worldwide, our customers have never had a wider choice of accessing their funds.

Easy payments and cash withdrawals: There is no need for our customers to carry cash on them when travelling within and outside the country as they can get access to their funds from our numerous ATMs as well as that of the other Banks. The GCB MasterCard Debit cards have simplified travelling as the customers do not need to look for foreign currency when travelling. So long as there are sufficient funds in their account they will be able to use their card and money.

  • Competitive Rates: The MasterCard rates are comparatively cheaper than the exchange rate at the Forex bureau.
  • Convenient Banking: There is also the convenience of using one card in Ghana as well as aboard. The customer does not need to change cards or remember new passwords with their cards. The same Card, Signature and PIN will work on all ATMs and POS devices worldwide.
  • Security: Since the customers do not need to carry cash when travelling, the card makes the customer's journey safer than when carrying cash.
  • Access to a 24/7 Call Centre: There are dedicated 24-hr customer help lines to which the customer can reach us any time of the day and anywhere in the world
  • Prestige: The GCB MasterCard cardholders are also associated with a world class brand. MasterCard worldwide is perceived as a premium card brand and that is what the customers get with the GCB MasterCard.
  • Cardholders have wider service options such as access to GCB internet banking.
  • Customer Satisfaction: GCB MasterCard makes life much easier and banking much faster and more pleasant, for customers.
